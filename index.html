Johnstone Cycling
Justin Johnstone
bicycle tourist · traveler · urban planner
Strava

The bicycle provides a unique means of experiencing the world. Fast enough to cover meaningful distances, slow enough to truly absorb your environment. It is also one of the most reliable ways to connect with people.

That belief has bled into everything I do. It led me to found NYC Bike + Brew, a weekly group ride that has since grown into one of the city's largest, with chapters spanning multiple cities. It took me across America in the summer of 2024, riding from New York City to San Francisco and raising over $10,000 for Doctors Without Borders. It brought me to Asia, where I set off on a year-long bicycle tour across the continent.

By training I'm an urban planner, someone who thinks about how people move through and inhabit the places they call home. My worked primarily focused on climate resiliency and finding solutions to weather related impacts.

This site is a record of my experience as a touring cyclists and community builder. The miles logged, the countries traversed, connections made, and the perspective gained from seeing the world in the saddle.
